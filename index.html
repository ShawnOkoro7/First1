What is sugar?

Sugar is a chemical compound that is made up of hydrogen and carbon. It is a sweet, flavourful, and nutritious substance that is widely used in many foods.

Why is sugar bad for you?

Sugar is a major contributor to many diseases, including heart disease, diabetes, and cancer. Sign our petition to help raise awareness and call for reduced sugar consumption.
